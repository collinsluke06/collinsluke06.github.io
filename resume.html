The Most Important Resume Sections

1. Header

  • Name
  • Email - use your University email if you are in College
  • Phone Number
  • LinkedIn

2. Objective Statements

Only include Objective Statements if you to fill up space on your resume. If you include one, make sure to keep it short and simple.

3. Education

  • University Name & Location
  • Graduation Date
  • Major
  • Minors (if applicable)
  • GPA

4. Experiences

  • Previous Internships & Jobs
  • Projects (Personal & School)
  • Club Involvement on Campus
  • Any Leadership Positions

5. Skills

  • Speaking Languages
  • Coding/Programming Languages
Do not include soft skills such as:
  • Time Management
  • Hard Working
  • Critical Thinking
  • Organizational
  • Communications

Format Guidelines

  • Page Length: 1 Page Max for Undergrads
    • Graduate students are allowed 2 pages
  • Font Size: 10.5 - 12pt font
  • Font Type: Times New Roman, Ariel
  • Margin Top & Bottom: 1 - 0.5in on each side
  • Margin Sides: 1 - 0.5in on each side
